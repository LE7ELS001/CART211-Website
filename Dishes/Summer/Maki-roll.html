Maki Roll
"You humans have the strangest eating habits. You put fish and vegetables on rice, and wrap it in seaweed? Odd, but tasty!" -DWARF
—Ingredients—

1½ cups good-quality JAPANESE SHORT-GRAIN RICE

1½ cups WATER
½ cup finely diced CARROT
¼ cup UNSEASONED RICE WINE VINEGAR
1 tablespoon GRANULATED SUGAR
KOSHER SALT 12 ounces skinless SUSHI-GRADE SALMON FILLET
3 tablespoons MAYONNAISE
2 to 3 teaspoons ASIAN CHILI SAUCE, such as sriracha, as desired
½ teaspoon toasted SESAME OIL
2 thinly sliced SCALLIONS
6 full-size ROASTED NORI SHEETS
1 large PERSIAN CUCUMBER, thinly sliced into matchsticks
1 ripe AVOCADO, halved, seed removed, sliced lengthwise into 18 pieces
PICKLED GINGER, WASABI, and SOY SAUCE
