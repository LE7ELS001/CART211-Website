Fish Stew
"This scent…it reminds me of my days at sea, sailing with my husband. We'd make big pots of seafood soup every time we managed to find tomatoes at some port or another. *sniff* To think I'd almost forgotten…" -BIRDIE
—Ingredients—

1 pound RAINBOW CHERRY TOMATOES, halved

3½ tablespoons EXTRA-VIRGIN OLIVE OIL, plus more for drizzling
KOSHER SALT
4 cups SHRIMP STOCK
¼ teaspoon GROUND CINNAMONor store-bought fish or seafood stock
1 large ripe BEEFSTEAK TOMATO, chopped
4 GARLIC CLOVES, minced
1 small RED BELL PEPPER, cored, seeded, and thinly sliced
¼ to ½ teaspoon RED PEPPER FLAKES, plus more as needed
1½ cups (6 fl oz) DRY WHITE WINE
1 pound STEELHEAD TROUT, cleaned, skin removed, cut into 2-inch pieces
8 live CRAYFISH or fresh large head-on, shell-on SHRIMP
12 ounces large PERIWINKLE SNAILS or LITTLENECK CLAMS, rinsed well and scrubbed clean
12 ounces MUSSELS, any beards removed, scrubbed clean
12 ounces MUSSELS, for garnish
Lightly toasted slices of BREAD
